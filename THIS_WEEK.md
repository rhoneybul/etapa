## What happens AFTER each post (the growth hack)

The algorithm rewards the first 60 minutes. So:

**0-5 min after posting:**
- Reply to any comment that's already appeared
- Send the post to 5 friends via DM asking them to "let me know if this resonates"

**5-60 min after posting:**
- Go to #cyclingbeginner / #womenwhocycle / #cyclingcommunity
- Comment thoughtfully (2-sentence minimum) on 10 recent posts from accounts with 500-5,000 followers
- Reply to every comment on your own post within 10 minutes

**60+ min:**
- Check the admin dashboard → Signups → filter by `source=instagram` once a day
- Note saves vs likes ratio — that's your real signal

**Don't:**
- Post and disappear. Worst mistake.
- Post all 9 at once. Kills algorithm distribution.
- Delete a low-performing post in the first 24h. Let it breathe.
