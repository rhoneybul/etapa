How It Works

From Goal to Training Plan
in Under a Minute

Tell us about your event, pick your settings, choose a coach, and your AI-generated plan is ready.

01

Set Your Goal

Enter your event name, distance, elevation, and race date. Whether it's a local gran fondo or L'Etape du Tour, we'll tailor the plan to get you there.

02

Build Your Week

Set how many sessions you can do, pick which days work for you, choose your plan duration and start date. The plan fits your life, not the other way around.

03

AI Builds Your Plan

Your AI coach creates a fully structured, periodised training plan with base, build, peak, and taper phases. See your total km, sessions, hours, and weekly volume at a glance.

04

Train With Your Coach

Follow your weekly sessions, view detailed ride instructions with tips on hydration, fueling, and pacing. Ask your coach anything — swap sessions, adjust intensity, or get race-day advice.

Transparent by Design

Built Entirely with AI

Every plan, every coaching conversation, every check-in — powered by AI and grounded in real cycling science.

AI-Generated Plans

Training plans are created by Anthropic's Claude AI, instructed with established cycling coaching principles. No human coach reviews individual plans — the AI handles everything from periodisation to session design.

Grounded in Science

Our AI draws on Joe Friel's Cyclist's Training Bible, Chris Carmichael's Time-Crunched Cyclist, Dr. Stephen Seiler's polarised training research, British Cycling pathways, and current sports science on progressive overload and recovery.

Evidence-Based Principles

Every plan follows: progressive overload (max 10% weekly increase), 80/20 polarised intensity, periodised phases (base → build → peak → taper), scheduled deload weeks, and injury-prevention through load management.

AI Coaching Chat

When you chat with your coach, you're talking to an AI persona. Six distinct coaching personalities — from warm encouragement to data-driven precision — all powered by the same underlying AI.

Know the Limits

AI cannot assess your physical condition, injury history, or biomechanics. Plans are guidance, not prescriptions. If something doesn't feel right, modify or skip the session. Use your own judgement.

Not Medical Advice

Etapa is a training tool, not a medical service. Always consult your doctor before starting any new exercise programme, especially if you have pre-existing conditions or have been inactive.

What to expect
You don't need to be fit to start — that's what the plan is for. We'll ease you in gently, building up distance and confidence week by week.
Nutrition basics
For rides under an hour, water is all you need. For longer rides, bring a banana or energy bar. Eat a light meal 1–2 hours before riding.
Rest is part of training
Your body gets stronger on rest days, not riding days. Don't skip them! Consistency over time beats pushing through fatigue.
