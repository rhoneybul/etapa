A weekly check-in that shapes next week's plan. Send any indoor session to your trainer. Pick the bike that fits your riding.

Weekly check-in Experimental

Two minutes on Sunday — your plan reshapes itself for next week

Once a week your coach asks how things actually went. You tick what you did, drop one line about anything coming up ("can't ride Wednesday"), and the coach proposes concrete tweaks to next week — apply or skip each one with a tap.

  • Pick the day and time in Settings → Weekly check-in.
  • One freeform input, then injury yes/no — the whole thing fits in a couple of minutes.
  • Your coach never gives medical advice. Anything injury-related gets pointed at a physio, full stop.
Send to your trainer Experimental

Any indoor session, into your trainer app, in three taps

Open an indoor session, tap Send to your trainer, and we generate a real structured workout file (.zwo). The fastest path on a phone is intervals.icu — free, takes mobile uploads, and pushes workouts straight into Zwift, Garmin Edge, and Wahoo ELEMNT.

  • Save the .zwo, drop it on intervals.icu, ride.
  • Already on Zwift, Wahoo SYSTM, Rouvy, or TrainerRoad? The in-app modal walks you through the per-app web upload.
  • FTP set in your trainer app once → every workout plays back at the right watts.
Cycling type

Pick the bike that fits your riding — swap any session in one tap

Road, gravel, MTB, e-bike, or indoor — pick the one that defines your training and the plan reads native to it (singletrack for MTB, mixed surface for gravel, structured indoor for trainer riders).

  • Tap the bike chip on any ride to swap just that session — same effort, sensible distance.
  • Changing every Thursday to gravel? It'll offer to apply across all upcoming weeks.
  • Change your default any time in Settings → Default cycling type.

27 April 2026

A clearer way to pick your plan. Drag-and-drop sessions. A chat that never drops a message. A brand-new coach.

Choosing your plan

Three questions and you've got a plan

What are you training for, what's your current longest ride, when's your event. No FTP test, no "beginner / intermediate / advanced" guess — your actual longest ride anchors Week 1 and we build from there.

Drag and drop

Move a session by dragging it

Press-and-hold a session on Home or in Calendar and drag it to a new day. Same gesture in both places.

Messaging

Coach chat that opens instantly and never loses a reply

The conversation is there the moment the screen opens. Send and close — when you come back, your message and the coach's reply are both intact. Push notifications drop you on the exact message they're about.

New coach & languages

Meet Kai — and chat with your coach in their language

Kai Donovan joins the roster — sun-warm, no-parody Aussie. And every coach now speaks multiple languages: tap the language pill in the chat header to switch (Spanish, Catalan, Danish, German, French, Italian — depending on the coach).

Home screen

Today, tomorrow, and your week on one screen

Today's session is the first thing you see. Tomorrow sits beneath it. The next five days run in a horizontal scroll with session-type tags so the week reads as a week. Coach chat sits at the top — never more than a tap away.

Onboarding

You meet your coach the second you arrive

Three quick steps right after sign-in: pick a coach, choose km or miles, then you're in. No empty-home-screen confusion.
